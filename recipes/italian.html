Italian Sausage Tortellini recipe

Home

Description

Brown 1 lb italian sausage in a skillet until golden and crumbly
then drain the fat. Pour in one jar on marinaa and 1/2 cup water, stirring it into a bubbling
sauce.

Add one bag of tortellini, cover the pan, and simmer until the pasta
is tender. Stir in 1 cup cream and fresh spinach until the
sauce is smooth and the leaves are soft

Ingredients

  • 1lb italian sausage
  • 1 bag(20 oz) cheese tortellini
  • 1 jar (24 oz) marinara and 1/2 cup of water
  • 1 cup heavy cream + 2 cups fresh spinach

Steps

  1. Cook and crumble sausage in a pan; drain fat
  2. Stir in marinara and water; bring to a boil
  3. Add tortellini, cover, and simmer 5 minuters until soft
  4. Stir in cream and spinach until wilted
