Meat lasagna recipe

Home

Description

Layer savory meat sauce with tender pasta and
creamy be`chamel or ricotta. Focus on
slow-simmered rag`u and high-qualit cheeses.

Bake until the top is golden and bubbly
then let it rest for 15 minutes. This ensures the layers
set perfectly for a clean, sturdty slice.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Marinara sauce
  • Cheese
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Brown 1lb meat with 1 onion; stir in 24 oz marinara
  2. Stir 15 oz ricotta with 1 egg
  3. Boil 9 noodles until soft; drain
  4. In a dish, layer (Sauce -- Noodles -- Ricotta --- Mozzarella). Repeat twice
  5. Cover with foil at 375 degree celcius for 25 minutes; uncover for 15 min.
