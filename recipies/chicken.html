Description

Season 1.5 lbs chicken and sear in pan until golden;
remobe and set aside. Saute` one sliced onion in the same pan until soft

Stir in one can cream of chicken soup and 1/2 cup broth. Return thec
to the pan, cover adn simmer for 10 minutes unitl tender

Ingredients

  • 1.5 lbs breasts or thighs
  • 1 medium, sliced
  • 1 can (10.5 oz) Cream of Chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth

Steps

  1. Brown seasoned chicken in a pan; remove and set aside
  2. Cook sliced onions in the same pan until soft
  3. Stir in soup and broth until smooth
  4. Put chicken back in, cover, and cook for 10 minutes
